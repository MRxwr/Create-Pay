FAQ

Where can I find you ?

We are in your Appstore and Android Play Store.

How to join Create Pay family ?

Install Create Pay app , click on register and fill the form. Its as simple as that.

How to create payment links ?

Open Create Pay app, login in with your account, click on create invoice, fill the form and click on create bill. that's it!

Can i share those links with people ?

Yes, of course you can, after create a link, just click on share button or copy the link to where ever you want.
